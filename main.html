Scholar

With a PhD in Creative Writing and over 15 years of experience, Amy has taught literature, acting, and writing across the U.S. and New Zealand. She recently served as Associate Dean of Performing Arts at Bethel College and is a sought-after speaker and coach.

View Resume

Actor

Amy Leigh Wicks has performed on stage and screen, from Shakespeare and Co. in Lennox, MA to features like Ghost in the Shell, Good-Bye Pork Pie, and Catching the Black Widow. She was previously represented by Kirsty Bunny Management and brings authenticity and depth to every role.

Poet

Amy’s poetry explores the intersections of love, geography, memory, and identity. Her works are published internationally and she has toured as a poet in NYC, Ireland, and New Zealand.
